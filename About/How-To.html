Connect To Beanstalk

In addition to an internet connection, interacting with Beanstalk requires:

  • A Metamask Ethereum wallet with an Ether (ETH) balance.
  • Connecting your Metamask wallet to Beanstalk.

  2. Click the circle to the right of your Metamask address.
  3. Click "Connect Hardware Wallet."
  4. Click either "Ledger" or "Trezor."
  5. Click "Connect."

  5. Congratulations, you are now connected to Beanstalk. Double check that your wallet address is the same as your Metamask wallet address.

Earn Interest on Beans

There are 2 ways to earn interest directly from Beanstalk:

  • Become a Silo Member
  • Become a Bean Farmer

The Beanstalk website facilitates Buying and Depositing directly from ETH, Claiming and Depositing, and Buying and Sowing directly from ETH, in single transactions. Users can also Buy and Deposit LP Tokens directly from Beans. In each module, there is a settings module that lets you customize your transaction.

Become a Silo Member

Anyone can become a Silo Member by Depositing Beans or LP Tokens for the Bean: ETH Uniswap liquidity pool into the Silo. Upon Deposit, Silo Members receive Stalk and Seeds. Stalk is the governance token of Beanstalk. Every season, each Seed yields .0001 additional Stalk. Additional Stalk must be manually Claimed to begin earning interest.

At the beginning of each Season, if the Bean supply increases, a portion of the new supply is distributed to Stalk holders in proportion to their Stalk ownership. All Bean distributions to Silo Members are automatically Deposited into the Silo, and receive Stalk and Seeds. Additional Stalk that grows from new Seeds do not need to be manually claimed until after your next interaction with the Silo. After your next interaction with the Silo, any additional Stalk from Seeds owned at the time of your interaction with the Silo must be manually claimed.

Assets can be Withdrawn from the Silo at anytime, and become liquid at the start of the 25th Season after Withdrawal. All Stalk and Seeds associated with a Deposit are forfeited upon Withdrawal.

Become a Bean Farmer

Anyone can become a Bean Farmer by Sowing Beans in Soil (lending Beans to Beanstalk). Anytime there is Soil in the Field, Beanstalk is willing to issue Pods (debt). The interest rate on Bean loans is determined by the Weather at the time of Sowing. Pods become Harvestable (redeemable for 1 Bean) on a First In, First Out basis.

Redeem Interest

Interest is paid automatically to Silo Members and Bean Farmers.

Silo Members receive interest in the form of Deposited Beans. Interest payments automatically receive Stalk and Seeds. Every season, each Seed yields .0001 additional Stalk. Silo Members can Withdraw their Beans and LP Tokens from the Silo at anytime. Bean and LP Token Withdrawals are Claimable at the start of the 25th Season after Withdrawal.

Bean Farmers receive Pods at the time of Sowing. Pods become Harvestable on a First In, First Out basis. Bean Farmers can redeem their Harvestable Pods for 1 Bean each at any time. Bean Farmers must manually Claim Harvestable Pods.

There is no penalty for waiting to Claim assets.

Understand My Holdings in Beanstalk

There are a lot of different assets in the Beanstalk ecosystem. The Balances section contains a complete summary of both your assets in the ecosystem, and the totals for the entire Beanstalk ecosystem.

Bean values are denominated in Beans. LP Token values are small decimals. If you hover over an amount of LP with your mouse, the website displays the Beans and ETH under that LP Token amount.

Bean and LP Deposits and Withdrawals are listed individually in the "Silo" section.

Whenever you have assets Deposited in the Silo and are rewarded with new Beans or Stalk, they will appear with your Bean Deposits listed as Rewarded. The next time you perform a transaction in the Silo, Rewarded Beans and Stalk will be grouped into a Bean Deposit and removed from the Rewarded section.
